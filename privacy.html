FAST Athletics – Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

FAST Athletics Track Club ("we", "us", or "our") operates the FAST Athletics mobile app and website. This page explains our practices regarding information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The FAST Athletics app is an informational app for our youth track club in Bryant, Arkansas. We do not require account creation and do not collect personal information from users. The app displays schedules, announcements, and club information only.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not contain advertising, in-app purchases, or user-generated content.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, payment information, or any other personal data through the app.

Third-Party Services

Our app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type) as part of normal web hosting operations. Please see Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: fastathletics26@gmail.com
